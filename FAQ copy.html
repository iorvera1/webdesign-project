FAQ

  • DO I NEED TO SEND DOCUMENTS TO BUY NUMBER PLATES?


    Yes, it is a Legal requirement!

  • CAN YOU HAVE A SLOGAN ON YOUR NUMBER PLATE?


    Yes, you can have a slogan on your number plate, however we would suggest that you use a lipped plate for this purpose if you are using your plates on the road.

  • CAN I HAVE MY BUSINESS LOGO OR DESIGN PRINTED ONTO A PLATE?


    Yes! We can do this in many colours or fonts or add your logo by means of a lipped plate.

  • DO YOU HAVE NEXT DAY DELIVERY?


    Your order will be manufactured and dispatched within three days, provided your order is placed before 1pm. As our office is open Monday to Friday, orders placed over the weekend will be processed and dispatched the following three working days.

  • CAN I SELL MY NUMBER PLATE?


    Unfortunately, you can't sell the registration your vehicle came with as standard, but you can if you want to sell a private registration.

  • TYPING ERRORS AND TYPOS?


    Unfortunately, you can't sell the registration your vehicle came with as standard, but you can if you want to sell a private registration.
